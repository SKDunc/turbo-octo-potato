Learning the Signs of Adoration

You see a lady and you have a crush on her. It's easy to get lost in figuring out what a woman is thinking when your judgment is clouded by your desire. So, what are some surefire signs that she's feeling you, too?

  • Body Language: Is her body open and her chest pointed towards you? Does she face you wherever you move? Does she lean in when you're close?
  • Eye Contact: Does she look into your eyes often? If so, does her gaze remain fixated a second or two longer than normal? Note: A lot of women use eye contact as their main tool of getting a man's attention.
  • Breaking the Touch Barrier: Does she touch your arm when close? Is she open to hugging you? Could you touch her shoulder, hand or upper back without shrugging you off?
  • Energy: Does she act nervous around you--giggles, constant smiling, fidgeting, touching her face, neck or hair?
  • How She Speaks: Does she compliment you often? Does she laugh at your jokes? Does she value your opinion?
